Setting: Tower! Elevator in the center, you gotta kill all the baddies to activate the elevator. Higher levels are harder (duh). Higher levels also get bigger.

Mechanics: First-person shooter. Levels have chests that drop random updrades. When you clear a level, you get one of three powerful upgrades.

Enemy types:
Melee - Small and swarmy, Big and strong
Ranged - Ground, flying

Player:
One gun and one ability
